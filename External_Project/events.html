Scenic Hayrides

Experience fall like never before with a scenic hayride through the heart of Pumpkin Patch & Co.! Our classic tractor-pulled hayrides take you on a relaxing journey across our beautiful fields and wooded trails, surrounded by colorful autumn leaves and crisp country air. It’s the perfect way to soak in the season with family and friends. Cozy up under a blanket, enjoy the ride, and make memories that will last long after the last leaf falls. Whether it's your first hayride or a cherished tradition, we can’t wait to welcome you aboard!

Pumpkin Picking

There’s no better feeling than finding your perfect pumpkin! At Pumpkin Patch & Co., you can wander through acres of vines, searching for the one that speaks to you — whether it’s tall, tiny, smooth, or bumpy. Our patch is filled with pumpkins of all shapes, colors, and sizes, so everyone in the family can find their perfect match. Don’t forget your camera — pumpkin picking here is a photo-worthy adventure and a special tradition you’ll want to relive year after year!

Group Tours & Field Trips

Looking for a fun and educational fall outing? Bring your group to Pumpkin Patch & Co.! We welcome schools, scout troops, churches, and community groups for guided tours of our farm. Learn about how pumpkins grow, explore our corn maze, enjoy a hayride, and of course, pick out a pumpkin to take home! Our tours are designed to be both fun and informative, giving kids and adults alike a hands-on look at farm life during the harvest season. Custom packages and picnic areas are available — perfect for a full day of autumn fun!
