Fresh, Locally-Grown Pumpkins

There’s nothing like picking the perfect pumpkin to kick off the fall season! At Pumpkin Patch & Co., we grow a wide variety of pumpkins — from big, bold beauties for carving to small, sweet ones perfect for decorating your table or baking your favorite treats. Each pumpkin is hand-picked at peak ripeness, ensuring you take home only the best of the harvest. Whether you're searching for a classic orange pumpkin, a quirky white one, or something uniquely shaped, you’ll find it waiting for you here. Celebrate the season with a pumpkin grown with care, tradition, and a whole lot of love!

Homemade Pumpkin Pie

Taste the true flavor of fall with our homemade pumpkin pies! Crafted with fresh pumpkins from our farm and blended with the perfect mix of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves, our pies are a cozy slice of autumn in every bite. Each pie is baked with family recipes passed down through generations, creating a rich, creamy filling inside a buttery, flaky crust. Whether you're hosting a family gathering, celebrating Thanksgiving, or simply craving a little taste of fall, our pumpkin pies are the perfect treat to share — or keep all to yourself!

Pumpkin Spice Candles

Bring the cozy charm of the farm into your home with our hand-poured pumpkin spice candles! Made with natural soy wax and infused with the sweet, spicy aromas of cinnamon, nutmeg, and pumpkin, our candles create a warm, welcoming atmosphere wherever you light them. Each candle is carefully crafted in small batches to ensure a clean, even burn and a long-lasting scent that fills your space with the comforting spirit of the harvest season. Whether you're curling up with a good book, hosting a fall gathering, or simply enjoying a quiet evening, our candles make every moment feel a little more magical.
