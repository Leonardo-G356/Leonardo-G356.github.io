Welcome to Our Pumpkin Patch!

At Pumpkin Patch & Co., we cherish the heart of what makes fall special — togetherness, tradition, and the joy of simple moments. Our family-owned farm has been a seasonal staple in the community for generations, inviting families to slow down, reconnect, and savor the harvest season.

Our pumpkin patch is a place to make lasting memories. Whether you're picking the perfect pumpkin, navigating our corn maze, or hopping on a hayride, there’s something for everyone. We believe in the power of shared experiences, and our patch is designed to bring families closer together. Watch your children’s eyes light up as they find their first pumpkin, or enjoy warm cider by the fire with loved ones.

We offer more than just pumpkins! Enjoy homemade crafts, fresh seasonal treats, and local honey, all made with love. Don’t forget our pumpkin carving contest — a fun challenge for all ages! And if you’re looking for a quiet spot, our picnic areas offer a perfect place to relax and enjoy the crisp autumn air.

At Pumpkin Patch & Co., we create a space where everyone can embrace the magic of the season. Whether it’s your first visit or your tenth, there’s always something new to discover. So, gather your loved ones and head over to make this fall unforgettable. We’re ready to welcome you with open arms!
